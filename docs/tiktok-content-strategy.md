# Cannabliss TikTok Content Strategy

## Brand Identity

Cannabliss is a curated music discovery playlist.
It is not an algorithm. It is not AI. It is not a bot.
It is a point of view.

### Voice
- chill but opinionated
- never explains itself too much
- speaks in vibes, not paragraphs
- treats music like a mood prescription, not a review

### Visual Identity
- hazy / smoky textures
- slow liquid motion or soft particle drift
- muted purples, deep greens, warm amber, neon accents
- no bright white backgrounds, no corporate clean
- text: simple sans-serif, lowercase preferred, slightly transparent
- think: the visual equivalent of what the playlist sounds like

### Never say
- "AI-curated" / "algorithm" / "automated"
- "check out my playlist" (too generic)
- "like and follow for more" (let the content earn it)

### Do say
- "added to cannabliss this week"
- "this one hits different at 1am"
- "if you know, you know"
- "cannabliss rotation"
- "stayed in rotation for 4 weeks straight"

## Three Recurring Post Formats

### Format 1: The Weekly Drop (every Friday)

**What:** 3 songs that were just added to Cannabliss this week.
**When:** Friday, shortly after the major refresh runs (after 6am CT).
**Length:** 15–25 seconds.
**Structure:**

```
[0-2s]  Hook text on screen: "3 songs added to cannabliss this week"
[2-8s]  Song 1: album art or mood visual + artist — title on screen
        play the best 4-6 second snippet
[8-14s] Song 2: same treatment
[14-20s] Song 3: same treatment
[20-24s] End card: "full playlist in bio" over hazy background
```

**Visual style:**
- Each song gets its own color-shifted mood frame
- Smooth crossfade transitions, not hard cuts
- Album art floating in haze, not flat on screen
- Song text fades in, not slams in

**Audio:**
- Use the actual song snippets back to back
- No voiceover unless it adds something
- Let the music sell itself

**Caption style:**
"3 new ones in cannabliss this week. which one stays?"
or
"friday rotation update. the last one is insane."

**Hashtags (light, not spammy):**
#cannabliss #musicdiscovery #newmusic #chillrap #psychedelicrap

---

### Format 2: One-Song Spotlight (Tuesday or Wednesday)

**What:** One standout track — either a new addition or a deep cut that deserves attention.
**When:** Midweek, between micro refreshes.
**Length:** 10–18 seconds.
**Structure:**

```
[0-2s]  Mood statement hook on screen
        example: "for driving through fog at midnight"
        example: "when you need to dissolve for 3 minutes"
        example: "this song doesn't exist in the daytime"
[2-15s] The song plays. Visual is a single slow mood loop.
        Artist — Title appears after 2-3 seconds, subtle.
[15-18s] "cannabliss" text, very minimal. No hard CTA.
```

**Visual style:**
- One continuous visual loop — smoke, liquid, slow neon drift
- Darker than the weekly drop
- Feels like a moment, not a list

**Audio:**
- One song, best 12-15 second section
- Let the hook or the vibe do the work
- Fade in, fade out — not hard cut

**Caption style:**
"found this at 2am and haven't stopped playing it"
or
"this is what cannabliss sounds like right now"
or just the mood statement from the video, nothing else

---

### Format 3: Hall of Fame / Survived the Cut (every other week or monthly)

**What:** Songs that have stayed in Cannabliss rotation for weeks. The ones that proved themselves.
**When:** Every other Sunday, or monthly.
**Length:** 15–25 seconds.
**Structure:**

```
[0-3s]  Hook: "songs that survived 4+ weeks in cannabliss"
        or: "these earned their spot"
        or: "hall of fame material"
[3-20s] 3-5 songs, faster pace — 3-4 seconds each
        Each gets: artist — title + brief snippet
[20-24s] "the playlist that stays alive" or similar
```

**Visual style:**
- Slightly more elevated — gold/amber tones mixed in
- Feels like an achievement, not just a list
- Can use a subtle "weeks in rotation: 6" counter if it adds personality

**Caption style:**
"these songs won't leave the playlist. rightfully so."
or
"cannabliss hall of fame candidates. argue with the wall."

## Content Calendar (Week 1 Template)

| Day       | Post            | Format   |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Friday    | Weekly Drop     | Format 1 |
| Tuesday   | One-Song Spot   | Format 2 |

That's it. Two posts per week to start.
Do not post daily. Consistency > volume.

If a post does well, post one extra that week.
If nothing does well, keep posting twice a week for 4 weeks before changing anything.

## 30-Day Launch Plan

### Week 1: Foundation
- Set up TikTok account (name, bio, profile pic)
- Define visual template for each format
- Record and post first Weekly Drop (Friday)
- Record and post first One-Song Spotlight (Tuesday)

### Week 2: Rhythm
- Post Weekly Drop (Friday)
- Post One-Song Spotlight (Tuesday or Wednesday)
- Watch what gets traction — save rate, comments, shares
- Do NOT change the format yet

### Week 3: Refine
- Post Weekly Drop (Friday)
- Post One-Song Spotlight (midweek)
- If one format clearly outperforms, do 2 of that one this week
- Start noting which hooks work (mood statements vs list hooks)

### Week 4: Decide
- Post Weekly Drop (Friday)
- Post One-Song Spotlight (midweek)
- Optional: first Hall of Fame post (Sunday)
- Review metrics: which songs got most engagement?
- Decision point: expand to IG Reels with best-performing content?

## Production Workflow

### Tools needed
- CapCut (free, TikTok-native editing, has templates)
- Spotify (for song snippets — screen record or use preview URLs)
- Canva or Figma (for consistent text overlays if needed)
- Optional later: RunwayML or Pika for AI visual loops

### Per-video workflow (should take <20 minutes)
1. Pick songs (from the weekly Cannabliss refresh output)
2. Screen-record or download 4-6 second snippets
3. Drop into CapCut template
4. Add text overlays (song names, hook, end card)
5. Add background visual (reusable hazy loop)
6. Export and post

### Automation potential (later)
- The cannabliss-engine already outputs which songs were added/promoted/held
- That data can auto-generate the song list for each Weekly Drop
- A script could pre-assemble the text overlays and song order
- Visual generation could be automated with AI video tools
- But start manual first — taste in editing matters early on

## Engagement Strategy

### Comments
- Reply to comments with vibe, not promotion
- "right? that one is unreal" > "thanks! follow for more!"
- If someone asks what playlist: "cannabliss on spotify, link in bio"

### Duets / Stitches
- If someone posts a song that fits the Cannabliss lane:
  stitch it with "adding this to cannabliss immediately"
- This is free discovery content

### Trends
- Only hop on audio trends if the trending sound genuinely fits
- Never force Cannabliss into a trend that doesn't match the vibe
- Better to skip a trend than to break the brand feeling

---

## Metrics That Matter (in order)

1. **Save rate** — people saving the video = they want the songs
2. **Share rate** — people sending it to friends = discovery working
3. **Comments** — "what's that first song" = hook is working
4. **Profile visits** — converting to bio link clicks
5. **Follower count** — least important early on, focus on saves

---

## What Success Looks Like

### After 1 month
- 8-10 posts live
- consistent visual identity established
- at least 1-2 posts with meaningful save/share rate
- clear signal on which format resonates most

### After 3 months
- 25-30 posts
- recognizable brand on the platform
- playlist follower growth measurable
- ready to expand to IG Reels with proven content

### Long-term vision
- Cannabliss becomes a taste account people follow for discovery
- The playlist grows because the content earns trust
- The TikTok is the storefront, the playlist is the product
